Privacy Policy

App Name: OhmniView
Developer: OhmniPath Solutions Inc.
Effective Date: 2025-12-01
Privacy at a glance:
OhmniView uses your device’s camera/album solely for on-device processing. Camera/photo images are not uploaded, not shared, and not stored on our servers. Any results are saved locally only if you explicitly choose to do so.

1. Information We Collect

Camera/Photo data (on-device only):
OhmniView accesses the device camera/album to capture images or live frames that are processed entirely on your device to generate results for the app’s functionality. These camera/photo images are not demonstrated, transmitted, uploaded, or stored by us.

Derived results (optional local storage):
OhmniView may generate derived data (such as processed outputs or results) from camera/photo input. These results are saved only if you explicitly choose to save them. Saved results remain stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

No personal data collection:
OhmniView does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the core functionality of OhmniView using on-device camera/photo processing.
  • To generate results requested by the user.

3. Data Sharing

OhmniView does not share data with third parties. Camera/Photo images and derived results are processed locally and are not transmitted off the device.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

OhmniView does not retain camera/photo images. Any derived results saved by the user are stored locally on the device and can be deleted by:

  • Deleting the saved results within the app (if applicable), or
  • Uninstalling the app.

5. Third-Party Services

OhmniView does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or tracking services.

6. Permissions

OhmniView requests the following permission:

  • Camera : required to capture images for on-device processing.
  • Photos : required to import images for on-device processing.

7. Data Security

Because all camera/photo processing occurs locally on your device and no data is uploaded to external servers, exposure risk is minimized. We rely on the device’s operating system security mechanisms to protect locally stored data.

8. Children’s Privacy

OhmniView does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app processes camera data locally and does not transmit or store personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
