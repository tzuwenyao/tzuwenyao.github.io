Supported Platforms and OS Versions:
OhmniView currently runs on the iOS and the Android platforms. Both phone and tablet devices are supported. Minimum OS version requirement is: iOS 18.2 for Apple devices; Android 15 (Vanilla Ice Cream) for Android devices.

Premium Features:
Basic functions in OhmniView are free. The following premium features require paid subscription: (1) Save Schematic (2) Save GDSII layer definition (3) > 10 devices for the built-in simulator.
